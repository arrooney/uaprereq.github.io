Project Involvement

AlbertaSat

AlbertaSat is a student group at the University of Alberta, we are a team of undergraduate students and faculty advisorswho build satellites!
Ex-alta 1 was not only AlbertaSat's first satellite, but also Alberta's first satellite. Ex-alta 1 has since left it's orbit, and lives on in memory.
We are now working on Ex-alta 2, an earth imaging satellite that will be designed to detect and predict forest fires using a multispectral imager and phenology.
My role on the team is as the Deputy Software Lead, in this role I have spent the summer with an NSERC USRA grant researching and implementing
an open source network stack for use on Ex-alta 2, as well as assisting in developing and integrating an Inter-integrated Circuit driver (I2C)
for the data bus.
Over the summer I was also tasked with designing and implementing automation for the Ex-alta 1 ground station. This was because operators had to be
awake at all hours to communicate with the spacecraft when overhead. I was able to create numerous bash, and FORTH scripts with hooks to C code that automated many routine telemetery collection tasks.
